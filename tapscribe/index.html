How It Works

🥁 Tap Any Rhythm

Press any key to the beat of a melody in your head. Tapscribe captures your timing precisely.

🎼 Instant Notation

Your taps are auto-quantized and rendered as real sheet music — beams, ties, dots and all.

▶️ Play It Back

Six instruments, a built-in metronome, and a note-by-note highlight so you can verify what you tapped.

📤 Export to MusicXML

One click exports your rhythm to MusicXML — opens in Sibelius, Dorico, Finale, GarageBand, and more.

🎹 Six Instruments

Audition your rhythm as Piano, Marimba, Bell, Pluck, Organ, or Bass with realistic additive synthesis.

🔒 Fully Private

All processing happens on your Mac. No accounts, no internet, no tracking of any kind.
