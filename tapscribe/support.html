Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tapping?

Enter a BPM in the bottom bar, press Start, then tap any key to the beat. Press Done when finished.

What note values does Tapscribe recognize?

Whole, half, quarter, eighth, and sixteenth notes — plus dotted variants of each.

How do I export to MusicXML?

After tapping, press the ↓ MusicXML button in the results screen. The file opens in Sibelius, Dorico, Finale, GarageBand, and most notation software.

The metronome isn't syncing to playback

Enable the Metro checkbox before pressing Play, not after. The metronome aligns to the audio start time.

Can I change the playback instrument?

Yes — use the instrument selector in the bottom bar. Change it before pressing Play.

How do I adjust the BPM?

Type a BPM directly into the field, or tap along and press Sync to lock in your live tempo.
