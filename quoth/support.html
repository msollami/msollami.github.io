Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?

Select a category or play a random quote. First, choose the missing word from the quote. Then, identify the author from multiple choices. You'll see your results and learn more about the quote's context.

How are quotes organized?

Quotes are organized by categories including Literature, Philosophy, General, and more. You can browse specific categories or play random quotes to discover new wisdom.

How does the streak system work?

Your streak increases when you correctly guess both the missing word AND the author. If you miss either part, your streak resets. Keep playing to build and maintain your best streak!

Can I save my favorite quotes?

Yes! Tap the heart icon on any quote to add it to your favorites. Access your saved quotes anytime from the Favorites section on the home screen.

Where can I view my game history?

Tap the "History" button on the home screen to see all your past games, including which quotes you got right or wrong.

How do I track my progress?

Visit the "Stats" section from the home screen to see your total games played, current streak, and best streak.

Can I share my results?

Yes! After completing a game, tap the "Share Results" button to share your score with friends.

Is the app free?

Yes, Quoth the Raven is completely free with no in-app purchases or ads.

Does the app collect my data?

No. All your game data, favorites, and stats are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What iOS version do I need?

Quoth the Raven requires iOS 13.0 or later.

I found a bug or have a suggestion. How do I report it?
