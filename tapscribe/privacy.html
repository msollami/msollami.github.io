Privacy Policy

Tapscribe  ·  Last updated March 2026

No data collected

Tapscribe does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data whatsoever. The app runs entirely on your device with no network requests and no analytics.

Everything stays on your Mac

All rhythm recordings, quantization, audio playback, and notation rendering happen locally on your machine. MusicXML files you export are saved only to the location you choose.

No third-party SDKs

Tapscribe contains no advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs of any kind.
