Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new node?
Press Tab key to toggle between fold/unfold, or use the keyboard shortcuts shown in the app.

Can I sync my files across devices?
OrgMode stores files locally on your Mac. You can use iCloud Drive or Dropbox to sync .org files manually.

How do I change themes?
Access theme settings through the app menu to choose from available color schemes.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. OrgMode is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No Data Collection: OrgMode does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.
  • Local Storage Only: All your notes and files are stored locally on your Mac.
  • No Analytics: We don't use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry services.
  • No Accounts: OrgMode doesn't require account creation or login.
  • No Internet Connection: The app works completely offline and doesn't connect to any servers.
  • No Third-Party Services: We don't integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

Your documents are yours and yours alone. They never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.
